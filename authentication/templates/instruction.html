MedBot Medical Assistant Instructions

Getting Started

  1. Open the MedBot application in your browser.
  2. Log in with your credentials or continue as a guest.
  3. Type your medical query in the provided input box.
  4. Click the "Send" button or press Enter to submit your query.

Features

  • Real-Time Assistance: Get instant responses to your medical queries.
  • Prescription Download: Receive AI-generated prescriptions and download them.
  • Chat History: View your previous interactions anytime.

How to Download a Prescription

  1. Ask for a prescription in the chat by typing "Download Prescription".
  2. Click the "Download Prescription" button that appears below the bot's response.
  3. Your prescription will be downloaded and also sent to your registered email.
